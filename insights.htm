Disruptive Technology for Fully Automated Medical Diagnositc
Mass-Market Applications

Most dangerous pathologies are not detected before becoming acute. The subsequent consequences are expensive treatments, mental and physical distress, long-term side effects and too-often loss of life. Serious illnesses such as cancer and heart disease drain not only the individual patients and their families, but institutions, governments, and society as a whole. While medical advances in treatment have made previously fatal illnesses more survivable, the costs are still unacceptably huge, and the outcomes need to be greatly improved.
The transformative, uniquely-proprietary FACTUS-99 Tensor-field Ultrasound Technology will enable routine real-time, high resolution, 3D imaging with deep learning identification and evaluation of early-stage organ and tissue pathologies. Most current medical ultrasound uses only reflected signals.  Through-transmission ultrasound carries additional information that enhances resolution and enables identification of tumors and tissues. Our new FACTUS technology will provide beautiful high-resolution 3D video imaging without CT x-ray radiation or slow, expensive MRI scans. The oncological deep-learning AI will identify and precisely evaluate small cancers at their early primary sites. The heart surgeon will know non-invasively in real time exactly where the patient has restricted blood flow.
